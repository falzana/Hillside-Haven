Welcome to Hillside Haven

Situated at the base of La Tourelle Mountain in Tamarin.There are two self catering aparments.The newly renovated and comfortable apartments open onto a spacious and beautifully landscaped, lush tropical garden. The apartments are situated at ground level, are fully independent and part of a colonial style house. The apartments are modern, clean, very well equipped, comfortable and very secure. They each have a private veranda which opens directly onto the garden. The gardens are regularly sprayed against mosquitoes.

Apartment 1

Self catering apartment

One-bedroom on the ground floor.One bedroom with a double bed or twin bed, TV, a bathroom and a kitchenette.Enjoy the beautiful view of the tropical garden, swimming pool, free WiFi and the sea from your terrace.

Guests have access to the communal pool located at the top of the property. The apartments are serviced from Mondays to Saturdays.
Tamarin Public Beach is within 3 km of the property, whilst Barachois Shopping Centre is 3 km away. Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam Airport is 52 km from Hillside Haven.

Note that a 50% deposit is required to confirm reservation. Payment can be made by bank transfer or by credit card. We accept the following cards: Visa / Master The balance must be paid upon arrival
